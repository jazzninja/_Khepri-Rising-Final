About

The writings of Khepri Rising are highly atypical and different than you would find nearly anywhere else in the world. A native of Southwest Massachusetts, Rising has adeptly borrowed from a great store of knowledge involving his own unique experiences and education. Rising graduated with a double major in Philosophy & Religion and Psychology in 1995. Since then he has been involved in building homes and roads with his father and the company of fellow tradesmen.

Having been a lifelong writer, Rising published his first book "Meaning and Relatedness" in 2007. The book charts poetically through his own life experiences in a Portrait of the Artist kind of way. The book in all was a product of 13 years of writing and editing. Rising next followed up with "Beadle Scat", a playfully slim book of poetry published again in 2007.

Having moved to the city in 2009, Rising explored a different part of his writing potential with the subsequent publishing of "Cipher Dios: Spirit Journey into Star Wars, Gnostic psalms of our time" in 2010, and the "Family Court Industrial Complex: and Post Traumatic Family Court Disorder" in 2011, along with "Cryptic Anthropos" in 2011, a serious yet slim book on the evolution of man through cryptic ovulation.
